# Item Type Allocation For 20 Categories

## Reinterpreting Existing Categories

- `large`: 场景锚点物。主物、承载物、成组物。
- `medium`: 主力清晰物。真实、独立、边界清楚。
- `small`: 小型可见物。第一眼可见，不用微型抽象词凑数。
- `hanging`: 条件物。有承载条件才适用，数量应少。

## Risk Rules

- Do not fill `large` with many near-identical containers.
- Do not fill `small` with tiny abstract label/sample/card/clip variants.
- Treat `hanging` as conditional, not mandatory.
- Keep lights, mirrors, transparent bottles, reflections, fine strings, tassels, curtains, and edge-line objects low priority or blocked.
