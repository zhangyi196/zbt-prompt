# Item Direction Optimization

## Main Insight

The item direction can still improve. The current `large / medium / small / hanging` categories are useful for compatibility, but they are not enough as content-authoring rules.

Use five content pools per category:

1. `core_items`
2. `support_items`
3. `visible_small_items`
4. `conditional_items`
5. `blocked_or_risky`

## Recommended Ratios

Default:

- core_items: 35%
- support_items: 35%
- visible_small_items: 20%
- conditional_items: 10%
- blocked_or_risky: 0% default draw

Outdoor:

- core_items: 40%
- support_items: 35%
- visible_small_items: 15%
- conditional_items: 10%

Special theme, such as `海底+潜水`:

- core_items: 35%
- support_items: 40%
- visible_small_items: 15%
- conditional_items: 10%

## Naming Template

Prefer:

- Real noun: `铅笔`, `水壶`, `潜水镜`
- Visible group: `金币小堆`, `彩色夹子组`, `贝壳堆`
- Clear container only when useful: `回形针盒`, `糖果罐`, `纽扣盘`
- Scene-specific object: `潜水标记牌`, `雨伞桶`, `野餐餐盒`

Avoid:

- `样片`, `配色`, `布标`, `纹样`
- `流苏`, `帘带`, `边巾`, `细绳`
- `灯串`, `镜盒`, `发光瓶`, `反光片`
- single tiny objects such as one pearl, one stone, one button

## Acceptance Checklist

Core item must satisfy at least 5 of 6:

1. Immediately identifiable.
2. Clear boundary, easy to circle.
3. Does not depend on light, reflection, or transparency.
4. Does not need special support, or support is clear.
5. Not a single dot, fine line, or tiny trace.
6. Strongly related to the category scene.

Small item must also be:

- grouped,
- container-backed,
- plate-backed,
- tag-like but large,
- or naturally clear in shape.

Conditional item must clearly state:

- where it hangs,
- where it sticks,
- where it wraps,
- or where it rests.

## Category Notes

### 桌面+学习

- Strengthen: notebooks, pens, rulers, calculator, stapler, sticky notes, folders.
- Limit: clips, tags, paper scraps must be grouped or backed by a box/card.
- Avoid: tiny samples, abstract color cards, paper corners.

### 海底+潜水

- Strengthen: diving mask, fins, oxygen tank, waterproof camera, sunken chest, shell pile, coral block.
- Rename animal-like items: `海星` -> `海星壳饰` or `海星形标记牌`.
- Use visible small objects: `珍珠贝`, `珍珠小堆`, not one pearl.
- Avoid: glowing jellyfish, light strings, mirror boxes, transparent perfume-like bottles, thin seaweed lines.

### Outdoor Categories

- Strengthen: backpack, bottle, lunch box, ball, hat, shoes, map, tools.
- Limit: leaves, stones, flowers, branches must be blocky or grouped.
- Avoid: one leaf, thin branch, tiny pebble, ground edge line.
