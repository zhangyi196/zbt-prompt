# Reconstructed Broad Scene Categories

## Naming Principle

Use:

`常见场景+活动/用途`

Avoid obscure or overly specific location words such as:

- 玄关
- 陈列台
- 边柜

Prefer simple, broad, common scene words:

- 桌面
- 餐桌
- 厨房
- 卧室
- 浴室
- 客厅
- 儿童房
- 庭院
- 门口
- 沙滩
- 海底
- 商店

## Key Changes

- `玄关+雨具` became `门口+雨具`.
- `陈列台+海洋` became `海底+潜水`.
- `书桌+阅读` and `学习桌+文具` merged into `桌面+学习`.
- `地毯+玩具` became `儿童房+玩具`.
- Added `浴室+洗漱` and `商店+零食` to cover common everyday images.
