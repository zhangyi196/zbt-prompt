# Challenge: Theme Matrix

## Strongest Objections

1. **Too many themes too soon**  
   Expanding from 14 to 27 themes risks doubling maintenance work and recreating the current filler-word problem.

2. **Style drift**  
   Convenience store, medical, tools, and sports themes improve image fit but may dilute the current warm handmade/home style.

3. **Overlap remains unless themes are sharply scoped**  
   Sewing, knitting, and linens overlap. Tea and baking overlap. Home decor, holiday decor, and ocean curios can all become display objects.

4. **Conditional themes should not behave like normal themes**  
   Wall hangings, lights, mirrors, transparent items require target-image conditions and should be low weight or tagged risk items.

5. **Some themes carry narrative risk**  
   Medical/care items can accidentally imply injury, illness, or accidents; they need careful naming and limits.

## Revised Recommendation

The best path is not a full immediate expansion. It is a staged rewrite:

1. Rewrite the existing 14 themes into concrete scene themes.
2. Add only 2 first-batch supplement themes: 文具学习桌 and 儿童玩具地毯.
3. Keep practical expansions as second-batch candidates.
4. Convert hanging/lights/mirrors/transparent objects into risk-tagged conditional pools.

This preserves theme flavor while avoiding uncontrolled variety.
